Reverse-engineering  of the TI calculators protocol
Guidelines

Tools used :
- DSP Starter Kit (DSK),
- or AVRanalyzer.

Analysis:
- fastAVRlink on COM2 (TiLP)
- DSK on the 'T'
- GreyLink on COM1 (TI's SDK)

Some offsets used by my DSK tools for storing data:
- CblExpmt: 31000
- Conics (acf2b3e.8xk): 22000
- ProbSi83: 36000 ?
- ChemBio: 23000
- TI83PlusOS114.8XU: 260000
