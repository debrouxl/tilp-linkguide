This directory contains schematic of the AVR prommer designed to develop (fast)AVRlink cables.
It can program both AT90S1200 and AT90S2313 FLASH microcontrollers of Atmel.

This schematic comes from the Elektor Magazine (french vresion), number ??,
October 98, page X-3. You can also find this article and its components at
http://elektor.presse.fr
